Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our shooting lessons and services

General Questions

What are the requirements to participate?

Participants must be at least 18 years old (or accompanied by a parent/guardian if younger), provide valid identification, and complete a safety waiver. No prior experience is necessary for beginner courses.

Do I need to bring my own firearm?

No, we provide all necessary firearms and equipment for our lessons. However, if you prefer to use your own firearm, it must be inspected by our range safety officer first.

What should I wear to my lesson?

We recommend comfortable, weather-appropriate clothing with closed-toe shoes. Avoid loose sleeves or low-cut tops. We provide protective eyewear and hearing protection.

Booking & Payments

How do I book a lesson?

You can book lessons online through our website, by phone, or in person at our facility. We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance for weekday lessons and 72 hours for weekends.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 24 hours in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be rescheduled but are not eligible for refunds. No-shows forfeit their payment.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for groups of 4 or more. Corporate and team-building events are also available with custom pricing. Contact us for details.

Safety & Training

How safe are your shooting lessons?

Safety is our top priority. All lessons begin with comprehensive safety training. Our instructors are certified professionals who maintain strict safety protocols at all times.

What certifications do your instructors have?

Our instructors hold multiple certifications including NRA Certified Instructor, USCCA Certified Trainer, and state-licensed firearms instructors. Many have military or law enforcement backgrounds.

Can I get certified through your courses?

We offer several certification programs including NRA Basic Pistol Certification, Concealed Carry Certification, and Advanced Tactical Training Certification.
