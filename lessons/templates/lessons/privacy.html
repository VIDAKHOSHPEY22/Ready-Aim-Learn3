Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Introduction

Ready Aim Learn ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personally identifiable information including but not limited to:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Date of birth and government-issued ID for age verification
  • Payment information (processed securely by our payment processor)
  • Training preferences and scheduling information

Non-Personal Information

We may collect anonymous demographic information and usage data including:

  • Browser type and device information
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our training services
  • To process payments and bookings
  • To communicate with you about your lessons
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • For internal record keeping

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who assist with our operations (payment processors, scheduling software)
  • Law enforcement or government agencies when required by law
  • In connection with a business transfer (merger, acquisition, or sale)

California Privacy Rights

California residents may request information about personal data shared with third parties for direct marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Secure servers and encrypted connections (SSL/TLS)
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

You may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and review your data
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@readyaimlearn.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be posted on our website with a new "Last Updated" date.
